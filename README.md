📊 Marketing & Lead Funnel Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes marketing and lead funnel data to understand how users move through different stages of the sales funnel:
Plain text
Visitors → Leads → Customers
The project identifies:
User drop-off points
Conversion performance
High-quality lead sources
Funnel optimization opportunities
A Python dashboard was created to visualize key marketing metrics and provide business insights.

Analyze user conversion across the funnel
Measure funnel performance
Identify stages with maximum drop-offs
Compare conversion efficiency

Drop-off Analysis
The highest drop-off occurs at the Visitor → Lead stage.
More than 80% of visitors leave before becoming leads.
Channel Analysis
Some channels generate higher-quality leads than others.
High-performing channels should receive more marketing investment.
💡 Recommendations
Improve landing page design and user experience.
Optimize call-to-action (CTA) buttons.
Reduce form complexity to increase lead generation.
Focus marketing spend on high-performing channels.
Use email nurturing and retargeting campaigns.
Improve visitor engagement strategies.
📌 Conclusion
The analysis indicates that the primary funnel bottleneck exists at the Visitor → Lead stage. While the Lead → Customer conversion process performs relatively well, optimizing lead acquisition strategies and improving marketing channel efficiency can significantly increase overall business conversions and revenue growth.
